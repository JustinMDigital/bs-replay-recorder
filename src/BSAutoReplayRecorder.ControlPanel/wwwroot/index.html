First-time setup

Set up Replay Recorder

A few short steps, then the recorder will test your capture setup.

  1. 1Beat Saber
  2. 2Recording display
  3. 3FFmpeg
  4. 4Recorder worker
  5. 5Test capture
Step 1 of 5

Choose the Beat Saber install to copy

Use a copy with BSIPA and BeatLeader already installed. Your normal game folder will not be changed.

Step 2 of 5

Choose the recording display

Start with one 1080p worker. You can add more workers and change quality later.

Step 3 of 5

Install or confirm FFmpeg

FFmpeg creates the video file. The next step checks your monitor, encoder, and NVIDIA driver.

Step 4 of 5

Create the recorder worker

Replay Recorder will create one separate Beat Saber copy for recording.

Step 5 of 5

Test capture before recording

This starts a short FFmpeg probe using your selected monitor and NVENC encoder. It catches driver or monitor problems before a batch starts.

Setup complete

Recorder setup is ready

Your recorder worker is launching. Import a replay when it reports ready.
